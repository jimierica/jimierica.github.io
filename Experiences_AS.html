Atmospheric Thermodynamics

This course will cover some basic laws of classical thermodynamics, including equations of state, work, internal energy, first law, second law, heat capacity, latent heat, Carnot cycle, Clausius-Clapeyron equation, and adiabatic rate. The core of the course focuses on the effects caused by the phase change of water and water, including atmospheric stability, buoyancy, available potential energy, gas block mixing, atmospheric heat map, and cloud formation.
