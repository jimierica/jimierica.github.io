Ecology

I joined a ecology camp, PROJECT ECO, which made me like ecology while I was 12 years old. In that camp, I traveled many places I had never heared before, such as A-Lang-Yi Historical Trail(阿朗壹古道) and Lanyu(蘭嶼). The teachers always taught us many cute animals and plants they had saw in anywhere of Taiwan. Therefore, I start to be interested about biology. Studing it in the high school, I had more chance and ability to learn about molecular biology. I also want to China Agricultural University to learn more. Althouth I am major in atmospheric science, ecology is still my hobby, and there are some pictures bolow.
