Study of Hadley Cell and ENSO

After using SST to analysis the connection between the strength and width of Hadley Cell and ENSO, we hope we can quantize the change of Hadley Cell's strength, width, and location, and confirm the conjecture. It's the other future work to apply the same method n the Tropical West Pacific, or swith the time domain to summer (JJA) or a year.
